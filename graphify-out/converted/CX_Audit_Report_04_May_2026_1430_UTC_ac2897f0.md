# 2. Persona: Kavita Meena - Basic Need Borrower – Medical Emergency Loan
27
Goal: Urgently needs ₹65,000 personal loan for her mother's cataract surgery at a private hospital after being told the government hospital queue is 6 months long
Constraints: Very low financial literacy, never taken a loan before, unsure if she qualifies without a salary slip, afraid of hidden charges, relies on her neighbor's smartphone
Lives In Jaipur (Rajasthan, India) - Pin: 302012  ·  Product: Personal Loan



## CX Performance — Dimension Scores

## Emotional Arc
Kavita arrived on the Bajaj Finserv homepage feeling anxious and desperate, immediately noticing the clearly labeled 'Personal Loan' menu item, which offered her a glimmer of hope. However, her anxiety grew on the Personal Loan page as she was confronted by complex fees, unfamiliar terms, and lengthy fine print that failed to answer her fears of hidden charges or offer plain reassurance, making her feel out of her depth. She ultimately stopped at the OTP wall, feeling unsafe about providing her number on her neighbor's phone, as there were no trust signals or supportive cues to help her feel confident in proceeding.

## Key Takeaways
- Kavita easily located the Personal Loan product from the homepage thanks to clear labeling and prominent menu placement.
- Jargon-heavy fee and term breakdowns on the loan page overwhelmed Kavita, who just wanted to confirm eligibility quickly.
- Critical reassurance about data safety and hidden charges was missing at the mobile-OTP wall, making Kavita abandon her application.
- Clear and bold CTAs, such as 'CHECK LOAN OFFER' and 'APPLY', worked well for Kavita’s urgent and low-literacy context.

## Friction Points
🟡  Medium Severity
- Personal Loan product page: Kavita felt overwhelmed and confused by the dense fee, interest, and eligibility explanations, which were filled with terms she did not understand and could not quickly scan.
- Personal Loan product page (fee section): Kavita searched for clear evidence that there were no hidden charges but only found small print and technical breakdowns, offering no simple reassurance for her first-time anxiety.
- Apply for Personal Loan (OTP gate): At the mobile number/OTP popup, Kavita found no plain-language explanation of why her data was required or if it would be safe, especially using her neighbor’s phone for the first time.
- Personal Loan journey (all steps): There were no visible options for quick help, chat, or a FAQ during moments of confusion about eligibility or fee details as Kavita navigated the process.
